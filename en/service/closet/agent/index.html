About agency recruitment

East Back Corporation is looking for a partner (distributor) for the Closet disaster recovery backup service.
Want to use Closet as your sales item?         
To apply for an agency, please use the agent application form .

Agency Benefits

Focus on sales activities

In the Closet service, we perform backup operations after the start of the service and support for maintenance support. Agents can concentrate on sales activities because they are not
busy with operations and maintenance support.     

About reward

Trouble handling fee

In the event that the Closet server breaks down and the agency replaces it, 5,000yen I will pay.

Reward for contract acquisition

When the agency wins the contract and completes the installation of the Closet server at the end user's office, 30,000 yen .

About the business to be performed by the agency

The following three points are provided by the agency.

Closet service sales and sales channel expansion

Closet server installation

Closet Server failure response
