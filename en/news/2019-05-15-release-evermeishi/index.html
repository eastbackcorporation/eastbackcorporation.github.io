Publish ever business card, a service that publishes your business card on the web

May 15, 2019
Share this: Twitter Facebook

Tag: 2019

We have released the service “ever name card” to publish your business card on the web.

“Ever business card” is a service that allows the recipient of a business card to see the latest business card at any time by publishing the business card on the web.

“Ever business card” allows you to create and publish a dedicated business card page. By pasting or directly printing the QR code of the business card page on the business card, the recipient of the business card can access the business card page.

In “Standard Plan (paid)”, in addition to the AR (virtual reality) display of business cards, a function to display an arbitrary 3D model has been added. In addition to those who can create 3D models by themselves, it is also possible to obtain 3D models from 3D model free download sites (such as Sketchfab ) and display them. There is also a “free plan” without the AR display function, so please try it first.

The “ever business card” has been pre-released in some parts of Japan and major cities in several Asian countries. Already used by many customers including India.
