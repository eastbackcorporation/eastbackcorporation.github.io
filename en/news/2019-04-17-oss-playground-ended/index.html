Open source software free demo experience site 'OSS PLAYGROUND' announcement

Apr 17, 2019
Share this: Twitter Facebook

Tag: 2019

OSS PLAYGROUND, a free demo site for open source software released on June 13, 2017, has been released on April 17, 2019.

It has been released as our iconic free service that develops business centered on OSS, but because a lot of other information is currently provided on the Internet, it has played a certain role, This service has ended. Just before, we have seen 20 people a day later, and we feel very sorry for the end of the service. The open source software hosting business will continue, so if you have any requests, please contact us individually.

Thank you for your patronage.
