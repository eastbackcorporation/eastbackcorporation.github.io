Privacy Policy

East Back Corporation is working to contribute to society through system development business, solution business, consulting business, etc. Under such circumstances, we consider it important as a social responsibility to properly protect the personal information of all customers and those related to the company, and thoroughly protect, manage, operate and use personal information based on the following policies. 　　　

Management of personal information

We comply with laws and regulations regarding the handling of personal information, guidelines and other standards set by the government.

Thorough information management

We will keep personal information accurate and up-to-date, and strive to prevent and correct unauthorized access to personal information, loss, destruction, falsification and leakage of personal information.

Purpose and scope of acquisition and use

We handles personal information within the scope of the purpose of use specified to the customer for business operations. In addition, we will not acquire, use, or provide personal information beyond the scope necessary to achieve the purpose of collection unless we have obtained the consent of the individual, unless otherwise required by law. We will also take measures for that.

Continuous improvement of personal information protection management system and system

We will continuously improve the management system and system for personal information protection.
